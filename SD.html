Our developers have been contributing code to major organizations and open source projects for over 25 years now. They've also been writing books, speaking at conferences, and helping push software development forward -- changing companies and even industries along the way.As consultants, we work on-site with our clients to ensure we're delivering the best possible solution. Education

Degree in Computer Science or related field Preferred.

Qualification

(Other notables: Python,Ruby, Scala, .NET, Clojure.)

  • Understanding and playing a part in the entire cycle of software consulting and delivery (ideation, project conception, UX, code, testing, deployment, and around again)
  • Agile. We believe strongly in agile delivery practices, having implemented them for ourselves and our clients for a long time. You should be very comfortable working with Agile, Lean and Continuous Delivery practices -- and love it as much as we do.
